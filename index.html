Welcome to my den!

Hello! Thanks for checkin' my site out! I'm KingPoss, or KP for short. This place has become my home and creative outlet on the net. Outside of messaging platforms like Signal & Discord, you won't find me anywhere else. Like just about everyone else on Neocities, I've become entirely disillusioned with social media, web 3.0 (and 2.0 for that matter), and the general state of the net in modern times. It feels good to stake a claim and make my corner of the web--take back a bit of landscape in this digital serfdom we're all serving under lol. Here, you'll find my art n pictures, writings, projects, programs, and a radio station I run with scheduled live broadcasts & interviews! I implore you to check out the music, learn a lil about me, and check out some of the other awesome sites here on Neocities. Oh, and there's a guestbook you can sign too! I hope you have fun lookin around!
