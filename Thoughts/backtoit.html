Getting back to it

It’s funny, despite being a PERSONAL site which I run with NO obligation to anyone but myself, I still feel like I’m letting someone down by not updating my site, or when I miss radio shows.

The fact of the matter is, no one really joins the broadcast unless I post about it in different chats (partially because when I actually follow through on broadcasts is so erratic), so there’s not much lost. Still, I wanna get better about posting more, if not for anyone else but for myself. It feels good to write, it feels good to create, but GOSH is it hard!

It’s not like I’ve been lazing around though! A good bit happened in the few months it’s taken me to pick the site back up. Donald trump was elected as fucking president, that’s something we’re still trying to prepare for...getting passports, securing our marriage, etc etc.

Oh! speaking of, My girl and I are getting married! We’ve got our ceremony scheduled for the 27th of this month, and honestly I couldn’t be more excited. Trying to work on writing vows. When we first started dating, she gave me a painting along with a letter she wrote talking about us. It’s super sweet and I can’t wait to read it, looking back on where we were and where we are now. She just lights up my world.

On the weekends and in between shifts at work, when we’re not running errands or lazin’ out together, we fish! We’ve got two solid lakes that are pretty close to us. We like to get there at or right before sunrise, but the frigid mountain air has made that a challenge as of late. Sure you can bundle up, but WHEW sometimes that cold just bites right through you. She’s been targeting largemouth, and I mainly find myself fishing for trout. They’re tasty, fun to catch, and the CDFW stocks them in both lakes, so your take can be pretty bountiful. It’s such a peaceful hobby. We’re new to it, but we already know we’re gonna be lifetime anglers. That’s partially why I’ve been so preoccupied and find myself updating my site less; being out on the lake is just too damn nice.

I’m gonna try to post more. I always look at what I write and feel like I’m rambling, but maybe that’s the point. Let loose, let the thoughts fall onto the page.

Guess the only way to feel like a better writer is to write more!
